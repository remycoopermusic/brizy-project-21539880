OUR TEAM

Krick

Christine Heitz, known by her stage name Krick, is a powerhouse singer-songwriter from Luxembourg who first captured hearts on The Voice of Germany in 2017. Recently, she won the public vote in the 2024 Luxembourg Song Contest with her song Drowning in the Rain. As the front singer of both the Northern Big Band and What The Funk, Krick continues to make her mark. Follow her journey as she dives into preparations for her first solo album release next year!

Remy Cooper

Remy Cooper; a sought-after music producer, writer, and composer who has worked with some of the biggest names in the industry. His talents have been recognized by international artists such as Watermt, Sam Feldt, and Martin Jensen, he has released on labels such as Armada Music, Found Frequencies, and Soave Records and created a remix for Lost Frequencies.

Remy's music has been streamed tens of millions of times, and he is known for his ability to craft captivating house tracks that resonate with audiences around the world. In addition to creating his own music, he also frequently writes and produces for various (upcoming) artists, using his expertise in electronic and pop music to help them achieve their vision.


With a passion for creating captivating and memorable music, Remy Cooper is a promising talent in the world of electronic and pop music.

Belgium
Udo Mechels

Udo is a renowned Belgian singer, songwriter, and new age pianist. Graduating cum laude in Communication Management from Erasmus University College in Brussels, he began his career in corporate communications before winning The X-Factor on VTM at 30, which led to a deal with Sony BMG. Soon after, he triumphed in VRT's Zo Is Er Maar Een, with his rendition of "Ik Mis Je Zo" becoming a Dutch classic.


Udo has since released multiple gold-status albums with Sony and Universal Music, and his award-winning songwriting spans top international artists like Jolin Tsai, Sandra Kim, and The Tenors, including over ten talent show winners across Europe, Asia, and Africa. He also scored Belgium's top-grossing film of 2020, Viva Boma. Inspired by French icons Michel Berger and Luc Plamondon, Udo now explores French music and ambient piano works under names like Ume Bod, while mentoring young Belgian talent. His ultimate goal is to win the Eurovision Song Contest as a performer or songwriter.

Udo Mechels

WHAT WE DO

International Writing Camps & Sessions

It doesnt matter whether youre an artist, writer, manager, or label, we can help with the creative side of music. We organize songwriting camps and sessions across various popular genres, including Eurovision, Pop, and Dance, with plans to expand into more styles in the future (K-Pop, etc.). By facilitating collaborations, we bring out the best in each talent and work closely with partners to ensure every project reaches its full potential in todays music landscape.

A&R Management & Development

We connect creatives with the right teams and sessions to bring their vision to life, offering personalized one-to-one guidance along the way. Our focus is on making the complex music industry easier to navigate and ensuring that every step, from songwriting to release, aligns with their long-term goals.

Partnerships

We work with a diverse network of partners worldwide, including labels, publishers, managers, and Eurovision delegations, to create impactful opportunities for talent. One of our valued collaborators is Greig Watts and DWB Music Ltd and their roster, with whom we frequently organize sessions and writing camps. Our partnerships have contributed to successes in multiple Eurovision national finals, including Germany, Malta, Luxembourg, Belgium, and Romania.
